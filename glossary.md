Glossary of terminology thattt
Hunt - counter press as soon as possession is lost if possible.
Press - recognizing cues or triggers to press and win the ball.
Compact - get tight together to control time and space.
Cover - support player pressuring the ball by taking away forward passing options.
Delay - get pressure on the ball to allow others to get back
Hold - Don't leave your positioning stay 
Shift - Horizontal movement as a team
Secure - establish possession on the regain. Don't force it forward
Disperse - occupy width and depth to create space.
Advanced Platforms - positive body shape and movement to allow for the opportunity to break lines and go forward.
Overloads - create and identify numerical advantages (+1).
Support - provide options near far and around the ball.
Balance - attacking positional awareness and defensive positional responsibility.
Set - soft backwards angled pass
Bounce - first touch straight pass back
Punch - to play your pass with pace
Time - when there is no pressure from the opposition to the player on the ball
Man On - Opposition is close to the player in possession of the ball
Turn - Change direction 
Drive - Carry the ball forward
Switch - Get the ball from one side of the field to the other

Recycle/Reset - Don't force it forwards. Go backwards and try again

Passing Angle - Make sure there is no defender in between you and the player on the ball

Skip - Play past an opposition line of pressure

Weight - How hard you pass the ball for the situation

Half Turn - Opening your body to see where the pass is coming from and where you want to go with it

Goal Side - put yourself closer to our goal than the opponent. Protect our goal

Touch tight - a defender should mark an attacker so closely that there is virtually no space between them

Check in, Check out - make a sharp run toward the ball (checking in) and then quickly pivoting or exploding into space in the opposite direction (checking out)
